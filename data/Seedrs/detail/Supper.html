Idea

Introduction

Supper is a mobile application platform aimed at giving working individuals and families a convenient way to eat nutritious home-cooked food on a regular basis. Our ever growing network of passionate cooks prepare their own home-cooked recipes for customers to order through our app and have delivered with just an hours’ notice.

We currently have 75 active cooks in our launch area of South West London all cooking their own bespoke menu. The only restriction we place is based on nutritional content as we aim to deliver not only fresh home-cooked food but nutritious meals. All dishes on the app are checked by our nutritionist for calories, fat, salt and sugar ensuring we are not just another takeaway or food delivery service, but an altogether new way of eating.

As a platform, there is no limit to the number of cooks or dishes in any location, and we have the platform infrastructure already in place to grow within our launch area and any other geography nationally and internationally.

Intended impact

In the UK there's a strong interest in cooking, healthy eating and home-cooked food. The Amateur Masterchef final last year pulled in almost 7 million viewers; Deliciously Ella’s healthy food guide, released last year, became the fastest-selling debut cookbook since records began; and many celebrity chefs are turning to home-cooked style food - John Torode’s latest book, ‘Recipes I love to cook at home’ being a good example.

So it may come as a surprise that in the UK, the amount of time we spend cooking has halved since the 1980s. We now eat more than 1.6 billion ready meals each year and a staggering 890 million sandwiches are consumed in place of an evening meal.

Why? – Convenience

Convenience now dominates our decision making when it comes to food. A recent worldwide survey concluded that 45% of people who buy ready-meals do so because they don’t have the time to cook whilst another 21% cited a lack of ability and 13% said cooking for one person was inconvenient.

So what if you could put all the good elements of home-cooked food - great flavour, freshness and healthiness and put it at the forefront of convenience? This is how Supper started.

As a platform we are looking to change the way that food is both supplied and consumed. We want to tap into the abundance of cooking talent, finding people who are passionate and cook regularly for friends and family and give them an opportunity to expand their hobby, earn a supplementary income and build their skill base. By doing this, we are giving busy and time strapped working professionals a way of accessing food that wasn’t previously available, that is nutritious, unique, fresh and high quality, all whilst being highly convenient.

We believe we're creating a brand new market in the food industry, combining our love of food, healthy living, technology and convenience into one proposition. We would like as many people as possible to come on this journey with us as we look to change this industry for the better.

Substantial accomplishments to date

Since launching at the end of August 2015, we have added 75 chefs contributing over 300 unique recipes for hungry Londoners. Our 5 star rating system encourages users to rate dishes and cooks after each meal and to date, 91% of all ratings have been 4 star or higher.

Our emphasis to date has been on building the infrastructure, logistics and proposition to be able to build a platform that is fully scalable. We already have an excellent record for processing and delivering of orders, as well as having been very well received by our customers.This solid infrastructure combined with a compelling proposition has meant that 64% of users have come back to us multiple times with some customers now using our service at least once a week.

The Press have been impressed with our proposition with glowing reviews from Time & Leisure magazine, SW Resident and Foodepedia to name a few as well as being featured in The Telegraph as a top health trend for 2016

Monetisation strategy

We work on a profit share arrangement with our chefs. Anyone can sign up to cook through our platform and we want to make the barriers and on boarding journey as simple as possible to attract maximum interest. There is no subscription or joining fees to be on the platform but Supper takes between 20% and 30% of each dish sold with the remainder split between the chef and delivery driver. Chefs also buy their packaging through us on which we make a small profit.

The app is free for customers with no subscriptions needed, customers just pay for the food they have delivered. All payments are facilitated through the app and automatically split between Supper, cooks and delivery drivers for seamless accounting and easy scalability.

The demand for convenience meals at home is demonstrated by the plethora of companies already in this space ranging from pizza’s to 5 star restaurants.

We are looking for investors to be our brand ambassadors and become strong advocates for what we are trying to achieve. We have ambitions to scale our platform nationally and internationally, with a particular interest in Hong Kong and New York. We want to create a new phenomenon but we need your support to get the ball rolling.

Use of proceeds

We believe we have a scalable business model that is ready for expansion so our priority is fully focused on adding more cooks and customers. As a marketplace, the more supply we have, the more people are attracted to it, so the first step for this investment is to build a stable and consistent supply of cooks in all of our geographic regions.

Once we have that in place, the majority of this investment will then be spent on marketing, advertising and PR to build the customer base to a critical mass stage to exploit the network effects.

A small proportion of the investment will be used to add some operational and customer service personnel to ensure we can continue to deliver great service to both cooks and customers as we grow.

We have some exciting plans and ideas for additional features and app functionality once our core market offering is established in the market. We will look to develop these in Years 2 and 3, when we will also look to bring in a CTO and Marketing Director.

Market

Target market

We have seen so far that we appeal to a broad range of individuals with a common underlying trait of being starved of time and looking for quality and convenience.

Our customers are:

a) Working professionals who buy food on demand - people now have busier working and social calendars meaning that food is now bought when required in the most convenient way. We have seen a good demand from professionals, typically mid 30’s who typically order from us on their commute home from work

b) Single person households – cooking food for one person is often inconvenient and expensive. Furthermore many food delivery services do not cater for individuals or will often insist on minimum spends or add on delivery charges. This is not the case with Supper, we cater for individual diners by ensuring delivery is always free and our prices start at under £10. With over 440,000 single person households with occupants between the ages of 25-50 in London alone, this market is one we aim to exploit.

c) Health conscious gym goers – we have done several collaborations with local gyms and personal trainers who are attracted to our proposition by the nutritional angle. People who go to the gym after work have little options if they want something healthy and convenient afterwards and with us displaying all the nutritional content for each dish, people looking for certain requirements from their diet can know exactly what is in their food.

d) Working parents - we have a good proposition for people who want to feed the family a nutritious home-cooked meal but often lack the time or energy after a long day at work. These people can use Supper knowing they can get home style food that is good for the whole family, without any of the normal hassle.

Our target market want a better alternative to ready-meals, freezer food, left-overs or takeaways but are not prepared to sacrifice on convenience. We bring it all, right to their door.

Characteristics of target market

Everyone has to eat right?

Let's not get carried away selling wild estimates based on the billions of pounds spent on takeaways, or converting everyone that buys ready-meals or grocery shops online. We all know this is a huge market so would rather concentrate on what we are good at.

Our simple mantra is to deliver a service that people want to use time and again through an app that people are proud to show their friends and family.

This is not to say we don’t know our market, we do. We know we appeal to working professionals who work full time, and are a solution for both working families and individual diners. We believe we can become a trend for the health conscious as well as the dinner party entertainers.

We are forward looking. Our app is on both Apple and Android and our website already boasts faster mobile speeds than the likes of Deliveroo and Uber. So let’s continue to deliver an excellent service in a high tech and convenient way and see how much impact we can make.

Marketing strategy

Marketing is essential to grow the platform and rightfully commands a lot of our attention and investment. A harmonious approach of both online and offline marketing with the accompaniment of continued PR is key to building the platform, underpinned by delivering good food and ensuring customers return to us.

We have gained significant experience in social media marketing since launching which is proving to be a good avenue for reaching our target market. People check their social media accounts throughout the day and on their commute and we aim to intercept people before they decide what they are going to have to eat that evening. We want to use this investment to expand our online reach, undertake SEO, remarketing and PPC activity to complement our social presence.

This will be complimented with our offline marketing. We have already attended several food markets and festivals with overwhelming support from people we speak to directly. There is no substitute for talking to people and sharing our passion and to aid this we have just taken delivery of a custom built tri-cycle which we aim to have out every day to use to engage with the public, offering samples and incentives to try us. We are proud of what we have and want to tell as many people as possible about it.

We also plan on teaming up further with local gyms, sports clubs and complimentary businesses to deliver a benefit for their customers. Participation in the community is important to us as we want to build a community spirit into our proposition.

A key marketing strategy for any platform is to drive word of mouth and for this we need to not only give great service but incentivise this behaviour. We offer 2 referral incentives, one for cooks and one for customers. Cooks who undertake their own marketing and bring customers to the app get an increase in the commission they earn, whilst customers can also refer friends with both parties getting discounts off their first and next order respectively.

Competition strategy

Where we believe we'll succeed is by combining elements of our indirect competitors and combining into one desirable proposition that doesn’t exist anywhere else.

1. We are at the forefront of convenience. We deliver food ready to eat and we can deliver at the time you want it, all through your mobile phone.

2. We are healthy – maximum thresholds for not only calories but saturated fat, salt and sugar means people can order from us on a regular basis and use us as a staple part of their weekly evening meals, not just a treat or one off.

3. We have an unlimited menu – being a platform, there is no limit to the range of dishes and cuisines we can offer and we can scale quickly into new geographies with minimal cost.

4. Unlike many platform businesses we have an added advantage in that remuneration is not the sole reason for supplying the market. Our cooks join because they love to cook and through Supper they are cooking their own food under their own name. This, along with our rating system, perfectly aligns their interests to ours in producing high quality food every time. With cooks using Supper for different reasons, this opens up the platform to a large number of potential suppliers.

5. We believe that our technology is market leading. Our platform, with rating system, filters, alerts and favourites offers a seamless and social user experience.

6. We cater for individuals up to dinner parties with no subscriptions or tie ins for customers meaning we can cater for the many households and occasions.

There is nobody we're aware of in the market that is offering the full package that Supper does. We believe that we have a unique window here to stand out and become a new way that people eat their evening meal. We want to become a new trend, a new community way of eating food and our platform will allow us to achieve this.

We don’t want to be the market leader, we want to start a whole new market and would love you to join us as we conquer this industry.
