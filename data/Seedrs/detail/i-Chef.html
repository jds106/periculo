Idea

Introduction

It’s simple – we connect the best catering and hospitality suppliers with professional establishments and Foodie consumers, via an engaging online platform and exclusive events.

i-Chef enables serious ‘Foodies’ to access the heart of the professional hospitality world. Experience 'Foodie' Red letter day experiences - visit recommended restaurants - attend private events - become part of this exclusive community.
Source fine food, ingredients and products from top suppliers.

i-Chef use the best of social media and mobile applications to enable members rapid product promotion, communication and acceptance of offers and sharing of ideas.

i-Chef is lead by 2 industry recognised hospitality experts and is supported by a panel of world famous chefs, and leading hospitality professionals, including Mark Hix and Giorgio Locatelli. The i-Chef panel provides a stamp of assurance and quality on our supply base, giving confidence to all users of the highest standards of products and service.

Intended impact

Whilst running the House of Commons catering, David was reviewing his hospitality suppliers and recognised that he had the benefit of being able to tap into a significant network of industry professionals – he just rang some of the leading chefs in the country to see which suppliers they were using and if they would share their information and experience.

The UK hospitality industry directly employs 2.9 million people, about 9% of the total UK population. Despite its significance, we believe that the supply chain is highly fragmented and relies very heavily on local geographic and personal networks, interspersed with few national wholesale suppliers.
Wholesalers concentrate on sales within the dominant catering businesses which account for a substantial element of the buying market, but many excellent specialist and/or regional suppliers have no economically viable sales channel to reach customers outside their immediate area.
Catering and hospitality operators’ value is through access to top suppliers - we feel that this is currently denied to the vast majority of them by virtue of their location, size, lack of experience, awareness or knowledge of them, contacts or time.

From our experience many outlets rely heavily on local or inherited suppliers. They are often unable to identify exceptional regional or specialist suppliers elsewhere.

One of the keys to most successful hospitality operations is good reliable suppliers with credible ingredients and calibre of staff - it is this strong competitive advantage that i-Chef membership provides.

i-Chef provides foodies with experiences and interaction that we believe is not available in a single forum anywhere else. Particular features are stunning “red letter” style experiences such as exclusive behind the scenes restaurant access and artisan supplier events. Foodie members access a community of world class chefs and hospitality professionals as well as carefully selected suppliers to enable them to purchase those same quality products available in the leading restaurant and hotels across the country

Substantial accomplishments to date

i-Chef launched in March 2014. In just 18 months we have signed up over 5,000 members with 200 or more new members joining each week, from both the supplier and professional establishment communities.

These members include some of the most well known names in the hospitality world, such as Marcus Wareing and Alain Roux.

Our Facebook site already has 6,000+ likes.

There are already over 16,500 products being promoted on our website.

We held our latest Golden Ticket event in July 2015, at the world famous Langham, where 200 suppliers, professionals and wider i-Chef community members spent an evening of networking, business and topical discussion, to critical acclaim and acknowledgement.

Monetisation strategy

The core of the i-Chef business model is the collection of annual membership fees, advertising revenue and recruitment fees from our various members, be they suppliers, professionals, foodies or recruiters.

Over time we plan to enhance this by sharing additional revenue from promotions and services provided by our supplier & professional members.

We adopt both a fixed membership fee and a ’freemium’ fee model.

Who are our members? Members are Suppliers to the hospitality industry, Professional outlets such as hotels & restaurants and individual Foodies. Suppliers are reviewed and approved for membership by a review panel of internationally renowned hospitality experts, including Mark Hix and Giorgio Locatelli

Why do our members join i-Chef? Suppliers join to promote their goods and services to a high quality pool of potential customers. Our site already promotes over 16,500 products. Professionals join to access an approved pool of suppliers and Foodies join to source the best products from premium suppliers and meet top personalities from the food & drink industry.

How do we grow sales and profits? We grow by building our membership and ensuring all our members receive incredible value and benefit from their membership. Each member has a distinct subscription structure, designed to provide excellent value for money and reflect the benefits received. Our sales and account management team ensure that suppliers maximise all opportunities to promote their products and that professionals and foodies engage actively in the community to source, discover, explore and enjoy.

What platform do we use to run the i-Chef community? i-Chef.biz is our inter-active community website and related mobile app. These create an engaging, easy to use online community, where suppliers promote their products & special offers and where events are promoted. Supported by a highly experienced team and backed by leading hospitality industry figures.

Use of proceeds

Investment will enable i-Chef to open up our community to Foodies, bringing a whole additional dimension to the business; our aim is to create multiple new revenue streams to accelerate our growth and strongly build our membership.

The introduction of an i-Chef App would provide yet more opportunities for members to interact with i-Chef - it would also provide an excellent opportunity for real time offers and incentives from our supplier base to be delivered directly to member’s mobile devices.

i-Chef will focus on increasing our membership through added sales resources and a highly focussed social media advertising campaign over Q4 2015 and Q1 2016.

Our key activities over the next 12 months are:
• Grow our membership levels by c.75%
• Introduction of a ‘Foodie’ membership in Q2 2016
• Introduce the i-Chef app across Apple and Android platforms by Jan 17
• Enhancement of our website to provide expert blogs and industry insight
• Evaluate International development opportunities

The company has an outstanding debt of £55,000, none of the proceeds of investment are intended to be used to repay this.

Market

Target market

Our addressable market consists of three primary groups:

(i) High quality suppliers who wish to reach a wide, highly targeted market place of Professional and foodie customers. These suppliers may provide foods, drink, capital goods or services to the professional and consumer markets

(ii) Professional establishments who wish to access the highest quality suppliers from around the country. These would include restaurants, hotels, pubs, entertainment venues, schools, hospitals and such like. These customers will also be our target market for there recruitment activities of i-Chef, as they seek out staff and advertise their needs to a highly targeted audience.

(iii) Foodies who wish to secure an inside line to the professional food and drink world and extend that to a community and experience based relationship. These Foodies would be individuals who seek out new and interesting food and drink experiences and who wish to explore sustainable sources of fine ingredients or products. They will also include individuals who wish to engage more deeply with the hospitality industry, joining special events, meeting top suppliers and chefs and networking to share their views and love of food and drink with like minded persons.

Characteristics of target market

Hospitality is the 4th largest industry in the UK, at £53bn. Within this are multiple supply chains involving producers, manufacturers, wholesalers, retailers and consumers.

Producers and manufactuers constantly seek new customers and to keep stock moving. Professionals seek quality products to offer exciting and compelling menus.

Foodies - those individuals who demonstrate a love and interest in food & drink and a desire to explore - are a growing force.

Viewing figures for programmes such as The Great British Bake Off (12.1m viewed the final in 2014 - beating the football world cup final viewers on BBC1) are evidential.

In the first week of September 2015 alone, 12.9m tuned into the Great British Bake Off (BBC1), 4.3m into Eat Well for Less (BBC1), 2.8m into The Great British Bake Off - an extra slice (BBC2), 2.4m into The Hairy Bikers Northern Exposure (BBC2) and 1.7m (per night) into the Great British Menu (BBC2).

Marketing strategy

i-Chef reaches its target market through;

(i) www.i-chef.biz - our on-line portal, with audience driven there by a combination of social media activities. The i-Chef Facebook page already has over 6,000 likes and our twitter account has over 550 followers.

(ii) The founders extensive network of suppliers and professional establishments. Between them David and Anton have connections and networks at the very highest level of hospitality

(iii) The i-Chef panel advisors' extensive network of suppliers and professional establishments - many advisors, each with their own personal and extended network of high quality suppliers and extensive professional outlets. Our panel advisers such as Giorgio Locatelli and Mark Hix are also significant personalities and will attract the foodie members to i-Chef.

(iv) targeted advertising and other media campaigns, including partnerships with complementary organisations

Competition strategy

i-Chef intends to address competition in our different target markets by;

(i) Providing a pool of suppliers to professional members and foodies that have been independently approved by expert panel members, thus guaranteeing quality, provenance and service

(ii) Providing Supplier members with a high quality addressable market of professional and foodie members who have been drawn to the community by the founders and panel and have been attracted to the by their desire to find products and services from a high quality pool of very special suppliers

(iii) Providing Foodies with a very special experience of accessing the inside line of the professional hospitality world, including the highest quality suppliers, famous personalities, exciting experiences, events and community shared interest

Our strong founders experience and network, plus our panel based approach, we believe give us a distinct competitive advantage over similar platforms or directory style approaches.
