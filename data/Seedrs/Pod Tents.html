Idea

Introduction

We believe we are the world’s first modular camping experience, we are exclusively designed and engineered in the UK. Totally innovative and created to transform the traditional camping experience, POD is a range of beautifully original tents that are built to be connected together. A social camping experience, users can POD their space with interconnecting tents to build a bespoke accommodation layout on the fly.

By reimagining the concept of form and function, we feel POD has solved a number of common issues with the traditional tent. Perfect for those who want to express their individuality, the POD is the antithesis of standard tents with the two sizes, POD Maxi and POD Mini able to be connected in a multitude of ways. There are no complicated set up instructions to wrestle with making pitching quick and easy and inhabitants do not need to crouch and stoop. Designed to be enjoyed, the POD Maxi and POD Mini unite seamlessly to give endless community opportunities.

Intended impact

We believe the POD is the first truly versatile social camping tent experience. It is the tent reinterpreted for a new generation. As much social experience as a way of enjoying the great outdoors, the fun of POD is that each unit can be joined together with any other, in any way, to build whatever the user wants. We wanted to put the fun back into camping and with this groundbreaking range, we think we have achieved this. We have worked hard to refine the design and construction of POD, incorporating features such as twin windows, higher wall vents and increased ventilation from user feedback to create a collection that we feel is second to none. Whether you’re off to a festival in a field or foreign shores, we feel that POD is an essential companion for friends and families who want to holiday together and have the option of privacy.

In addition to its external adaptability, the POD Maxi is also internally flexible with an interior that can be created, adjusted and personally designed on demand. An eight man tent, it can sleep eight people spaciously in an open plan sleeping area or, be configured to sleep four with personal sleeping cells and communal living space.

Substantial accomplishments to date

After a successful 2015 season we are now looking to improve the product further with the Elite range for 2016. The POD has design rights in the UK and all of Europe with a Patent of Functionality and Design in China. In addition POD is a registered Trade mark in the UK, Europe, China & USA.

We have been featured on The Channel 5 " Gadget Show " and have appeared on a number of high profile blogs as "a must have item".

We recently exhibited at the Outdoor trade show in Friedrichshafen Germany showing off a sample of the Elite model and as a result we are currently in discussions with some major retailers in America, Australia, South Africa and Denmark.

Monetisation strategy

Now that we feel we have proved a need and want for the POD product in the UK we are looking to expand to a global market place by identifying and allocating worldwide distribution partners / sales agents / retailers.

Our aim is that the chosen partners will have products shipped directly from our Chinese manufacturing partner to their regions and from there they will sell and distribute the POD brand. We will continue to sell directly to consumers in the UK and to the partners we already have in the UK including Halfords & Amazon along with global customers requiring products where we do not have an allocated location partners.

If a partners volumes warrant a discussion on exclusivity we are prepared to issue exclusive distribution rights for POD based on sales targets being achieved.

In locations where possible high import duties and currency volatility makes the product un-competitive we will also discuss the possibility of manufacturing the product under licence .

In addition to retail customers we are also looking to promote the POD range as a perfect solution for humanitarian / emergency / disaster relief agencies, military and for promotional / marketing companies.

Use of proceeds

Your investment will go towards the 2016 stock order of the POD Elite range - £55-£60K .

£25K will go towards exhibiting at the OutDoor - Show in Friedrichshafen (July 2016) and OutDoorRetailer, the world's largest outdoor retail show in the United Sates - Salt Lake City ( August 2016 ) .

£15K - To run a sustained global advertising campaign and product give away across social media and traditional print media to increase awareness of the POD brand.

Market

Target market

1. Recreational Camping tents - Camping has become cool again and families are seeing it as an experience for their families when holidays abroad prove too expensive. We feel that there has been an increase in good quality campsites meaning that families are camping multiple times throughout the summer and the ability of POD to upscale easily depending on numbers means you can just take the POD Mini if only some the group are camping or the Maxi and Mini's if your group increases in numbers.

2. Festivals - There are 2 possibilities for us in this market sector, one is for the pre-pitched market where companies offer a pre-erected solution for customers to arrive at a festival with their tent all set up and ready to go. The flexibility of POD means they can upscale easily depending on a customers group size by connecting the required POD Maxi or POD Mini's together for their clients whilst only have to stock 2 different tents for endless potential group sizing. Secondly we feel the festival camper love the POD as it allows them a huge social space in the POD Maxi while they retire to the POD Mini's to sleep in all without having to walk outside and risk the rain.

3. Disaster Relief -The functionality of connecting tents together allows for large families to be housed together and still be connected. They are light and easily erected and durable for a quick short term housing requirement. People can also be funneled through a POD set up should treatment / registration / inspections be required

4. Scouting / Group events / Sporting - We have already supplied some POD's to a scouting organisation and the feedback from them has been that they are perfect for the leaders to sleep in the Maxi and the scouts in the Mini's again safe in the knowledge they are only tunnel away. Anyone who has attended a children's sporting event knows it's tough to keep all the kids together in one place and dry / out of the sun. The POD Maxi solves all those problems by easily containing them in one space.

Characteristics of target market

We believe that the markets identified above are all looking for someone new and different. The camping market place has been offering people the same reworked ideas for years and camp sites are a wash with similar looking blue and green tents.

POD breaks that mould in many ways, firstly the Black tent measuring 5 Mtrs in diameter and standing 2.7 Mtrs high means it stands out from the crowd. Our experience has shown us that as soon as the tent is set up, people come over to ask about it. We believe that now is the time to offer customers a real alternative and we feel that our sales throughout 2015 and the number of global enquirers has confirmed the product has a global potential.

According to www.thestreet.com the global camping equipment market is to exceed $5 Billion by 2019.
The major drivers of camping equipment are increasing recreational expenditure, changing lifestyle, and rising participation in outdoor recreational activities.

Marketing strategy

1. Direct Online through our company website www.podtents.com - Profit margins are at their highest when we sell directly and are able to respond quickly and effectively to customers at a direct level.

2. Agents / Resellers and Retailers- This will be for a global market place as we cannot have a direct presence in all countries so selecting the right partner is critical, under this business model we will look to ship FOB (free on board) from China rather than having any distribution from the UK stock holding.

3. Amazon.com / Halfords.com - Whilst this is an expensive route to market, the exposure and customer base open to us via the Amazon & Halford portal means we should consider their route to market. We are in discussions with Amazon currently with regards to the direct ship and Amazon stocked models and have been supplying Halfords on a direct shipment basis.

4. Social Media engagement - By having a good social media presence we can have the POD brand shared and talked about all over the world and this drives customers to our preferred direct sale option.

5. Good old fashioned hard work - tradeshows, mail shots, email marketing and picking up the phone and calling people from trade directories or leads.

Competition strategy

We have always aimed for POD to lead the market rather than follow it and in the all new POD Elite range we have continued to up our game and taken customers comments on board from the initial first generation POD's.

We set the standards of our products at the very top end of the market in both the materials we use, the workmanship and inspection process, all our tents are independently inspected to BSENISO5912:2011 and CPAI-84 to ensure our customers receive a product that exceeds their expectations.

We believe that there is nothing like the POD range currently available in the global market place and the protections we have in place ensure that our unique design cannot be easily replicated ensuring we have many years of market share ahead.

We feel that no other tent company has a product that empowers customers to build a unique camping experience in this way. If your family or social group grows you simply buy another POD to add on, you don't need to throw away your old tent and buy a bigger one. Friends can come together with their own POD's and connect together on site in more ways than one.

Working closely with our factory we are constantly looking at how we can develop the product further and make it connectable and usable in other markets, connecting to a VW camper via a tunnel for instance and for a high performance expedition version.
